The Story of João's Garage

João's love story with cars and motorcycles began in the thrilling context of rally and motocross racing. From an early age, he was surrounded by the sights and sounds of roaring engines, learning the finer points of mechanics and vehicle tuning from seasoned enthusiasts. As he grew older, his passion for cars intensified, fueling his ambition to one day set up his own workshop.

After completing his studies in Mechanical Engineering, João made his dream come true by founding "João's Garage", a welcoming haven for car and motorcycle aficionados. Specializing in the meticulous restoration of classic cars and the expert servicing of high-performance vehicles, João's workshop quickly became a community cornerstone. With his reputation for excellence stretching far and wide, local aficionados and professional racers alike call on João's skills. João's journey from amazed spectator of local races to proud owner of a thriving workshop is a testament to the importance of following his passion and preserving the automotive heritage in the heart of Portugal.
